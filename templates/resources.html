Inspiration & Resources

Check out some of our inspirations in creating this site and resources for learning more about our mycelium mates!

  • Paul Stamets

    This well-known PNW Mycologist and author is everywhere - a must-listen for everyone in our opinion. If you're here, chances are we're preaching to the choir. He is a wealth of information about the various physical health, mental health, and environmental benefits of MANY different kinds of mushrooms and their mycelium. His website fungi.com has all sorts of great products and information. Look him up on YouTube or wherever you get your podcasts. He has been a guest on the Tim Ferriss Show, The Joe Rogan Experience, Bulletproof Radio with Dave Asprey, and more.

    This book is incredible.
